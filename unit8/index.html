    • Light Echoes

      The Barbican

      Los Angeles-based digital artists Aaron Koblin and Ben Tricklebank present a brand new laser commission, Light Echoes. Enter a new world and watch as a single laser projects a sculptural wall of light around the space of The Curve. Moving slowly, frame by frame, follow the light to its finale, a stunning visual rendering of data transformed into a wordscape.
